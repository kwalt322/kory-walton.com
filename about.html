Who is Kory?

Starting out in electrical engineering, I have always loved tinkering with technology and coding. I enjoy discovering how things work and often take the hard way out of situations in order to achieve a better understanding of how things function.

I believe that form should follow function, but function should have a pleasing form. That is why I decided to pursue interactive and graphic design as well as engineering. I love working on a project from start to finish. From creative to development. I believe I have finally found my niche and cannot wait to start a career in design and development.

When I am not designing or developing, I am working on personal projects, such as my single sticker I sell online to car fanatics {here}, or going to Hackathons or Startup Weekends, or adventure-ing with my dog Leela {@lezmobile}, or playing poker.
