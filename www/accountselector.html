Account Selector

This page was built to be an overview of all checking accounts offered by M&T. Traffic to the page was driven by email and banner promotions. I was responsible for the static carve and worked with the creative team to determine animations and page interactions. I was also responsible for maintaining ADA compliance throughout development. Once finished, the project was integrated into Umbraco to allow the easy addition of accounts and regional offerings.

  • Umbraco CMS Integration
  • Gulp / Harp Local Build
  • ION Interactive CMS Coupon Integration
