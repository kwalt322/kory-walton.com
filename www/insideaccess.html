Inside Access

Inside Access was built to provide United customers and Chase cardholders a single place where they could redeem their points. I was responsible for the static carve of the creative received from the design team. I worked with the back end team to handle the content being pulled in from various databases and APIs, while keeping a consistent user experience.

  • Umbraco CMS Integration
  • Gulp / Harp Local Build
  • Bootstrap 4

Developing for Compliance

For legal reasons, the client needed the entire tile to be hoverable, but also include various icons to be centered below text of variable length. From a developer perspective, this was a fun problem to solve because it allowed me to use flex-box for the first time.

There also needed to be the ability to change the "Sold Out" banner to "Now Available" or "Coming Soon". This little tile needed to accomodate so may small changes on the fly.
