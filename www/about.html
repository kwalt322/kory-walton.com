About

Starting out in electrical engineering, I have always loved tinkering with technology and coding. I enjoy discovering how things work and often take the hard way out of situations in order to achieve a better understanding of how things function.

I believe that form should follow function, but function should have a pleasing form. That is why I decided to pursue interactive and graphic design as well as engineering. I love seeing a project from start to finish. From concept to creation. I believe it is integral to any successful product or campaign.
