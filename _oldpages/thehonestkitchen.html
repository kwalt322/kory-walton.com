2016 Honest Pets Calendar Campaign

Site Design • Email Design & Code • SVG Animations & Embellishments

While working with the wonderful digital team at Red Tettemer O'Connell and Partners, I was responsible for designing The Honest Kitchen's 2016 Honest Pet Calendar Campaign Website. Based off of The Honest Kitchen's new site comps, I was tasked with designing a site where customers could upload photos of their pets accompanied by embarassing confessions. The campaign also included emails as well as a few fun animations.
